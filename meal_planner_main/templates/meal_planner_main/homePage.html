Why Cook-it?

Cook-it is a health and fitness app built for your lifestyle! Ensuring that you have the food you need, when you need it is our goal. With Cook-it you can quickly and easily find recipes with the ingredients you already have at home. That means minimizing waste and saving money! Not only that, Cook-it can also help you meal plan and find new recipes. Eating delicious new meals couldn't be easier!

How to use Cook-it

Using Cook-it is easy!

  1. Register with your name and email
  2. Visit your profile and add any dietary restrictions
  3. Add any ingredients you already have to your pantry
And just like that you're ready to get cooking! From here you can look for new recipes (or add your own) in the recipes tab or see how your friends are doing in the forum.
